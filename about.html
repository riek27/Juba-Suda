About Us

Transforming sanitation in South Sudan through innovation and sustainability

Our Story

Founded in 2018 in Juba, South Sudan, Quality Hub for Bio-Digester Services emerged from a vision to address the critical sanitation challenges facing our community. Our founders, experienced environmental engineers, recognized the need for sustainable waste management solutions that could improve public health while protecting our precious environment.

Starting with a small team of dedicated professionals, we've grown to become South Sudan's leading provider of advanced bio-digester technology, serving hundreds of satisfied clients across residential, commercial, and institutional sectors.

Our journey is rooted in a deep commitment to our community and a passion for environmental stewardship. We believe that everyone deserves access to clean, safe, and sustainable sanitation solutions.

Our Mission

To revolutionize sanitation in South Sudan by providing innovative, eco-friendly bio-digester solutions that improve public health, protect the environment, and create sustainable communities for future generations.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of sustainable sanitation solutions in East Africa, setting new standards for environmental engineering and community development through continuous innovation and excellence.

Our Focus Areas

Committed to excellence in sanitation, sustainability, and innovation

Sanitation Excellence

We prioritize public health through advanced sanitation solutions that eliminate contamination risks and create cleaner living environments for communities across South Sudan.

Sustainability Commitment

Our eco-friendly approach minimizes environmental impact while maximizing resource recovery, contributing to a greener, more sustainable future for our nation.

Technological Innovation

We continuously invest in research and development to bring the latest bio-digester technologies to South Sudan, ensuring our clients benefit from cutting-edge solutions.

Based in Juba, Serving All South Sudan

Headquartered in Hai Referendum, Juba, we're strategically positioned to serve communities across South Sudan. Our location allows us to understand local needs and provide tailored solutions that work in our unique environmental conditions.

From our base in the capital, we've expanded our services to reach communities throughout the country, bringing sustainable sanitation solutions to both urban and rural areas.
