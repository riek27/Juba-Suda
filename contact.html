What maintenance is required?

Bio-digesters require minimal maintenance. We recommend an annual check-up to ensure optimal performance, which we provide as part of our maintenance packages.

Are your systems suitable for all soil types?

Yes, we design systems specifically for South Sudanese soil conditions. Our site assessment includes soil testing to ensure proper system design.
