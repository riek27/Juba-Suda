Who We Are

Building Stronger Communities Through Peace and Development

Peace and Development Organization (PDO) is a registered non-profit dedicated to transforming lives in South Sudan. We bridge the gap between humanitarian relief and long-term development, ensuring that vulnerable populations have the tools, resources, and support needed to build self-reliant futures.

Learn More About Us

Mission

To foster sustainable peace and development through community-led initiatives across South Sudan.

Vision

A resilient, peaceful, and prosperous South Sudan where every community thrives with dignity.

Core Values

Integrity, inclusivity, transparency, and unwavering commitment to human dignity.

Our Approach

Collaborative, evidence-based, and culturally sensitive methodologies tailored to local needs.
