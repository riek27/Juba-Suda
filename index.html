Who We Are

Nyinkuei General Trading Co. Ltd is a diversified service company based in Juba, South Sudan, offering a wide range of solutions across multiple industries including cleaning, construction, logistics, and general trading.

Founded with a vision to become a leading multi-service company in South Sudan, we have grown to serve hundreds of clients across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and efficiency has earned us the trust of businesses and institutions throughout the region.
